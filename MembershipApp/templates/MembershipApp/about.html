Bubbly Ever After is a positive heart-centered global community of Bubbly Happiness Agents made up of incredible citizens of the world, burners, activists and techies embody the 10 Principles of Burning Man in the world that seek to empower people to feel better about themselves in a world that they are helping to improve.
Bubbly Ever After loves creating safe nurturing community, experiences, and spaces to gather and connect.
We seek to provide healing, affirmation, abundance, love and authentic connection for our minds, bodies, souls, and health at Black Rock City and beyond.
We began by hosting Bubbly Picnics in 2017 to bring people together and we've continued to host gatherings that bring together inspiring caring individuals. Come join us at our next gathering to meet our members and #StayBubbly.
We invite you to please share, create, stretch, grow, hold space and be bubbly ​with us.
Together, in love, care, respect and light.
​
Let's be a part of shaping the kind of world we want to live in.-BEA
